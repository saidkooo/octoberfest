A festival with two hundred years
of tradition

The festival has long won worldwide recognition and is awarded a variety
of epithets. In the eyes of some, this is one of the most popular festivities
in the world, for others it is a German national tradition, still others even call
this festival the most ambitious beer spree of the year. Millions of beer
connoisseurs from all over the world gather in Munich to pay tribute
to this noble drink. Read more>
